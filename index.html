Privacy Policy for LinkedIn Job Post Analyzer

Last Updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for using the LinkedIn Job Post Analyzer ("Extension"). This privacy policy outlines how we handle user data and disclaimers regarding AI-generated content.

2. No Data Collection or Storage

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This extension:

  • Does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
  • Does not track your browsing history or interactions.
  • Does not require account registration or authentication.
  • Does not send or store any user-inputted data on external servers.

3. How the Extension Works

  • Users can paste job post content into the extension for analysis.
  • The extension uses OpenAI's API to assess whether a job post is genuine or fake.
